Spaghetti Carbonara

Time: 30 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or Guanciale 150g
Eggs 4
Grated Pecorino Romano Cheese 1 cup
Black Pepper To taste

Details:

  • Authentic Italian pasta dish
  • Rich and creamy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta in less water than you usually use. Instead of tipping away lots of water after cooking, you’ll end up with a small amount of very starchy water in the pan – this will help thicken any pasta sauce it is added to, and will put the flavour lost in the water back into the sauce (recipes often tell you to reserve some pasta water to do this).
  2. You will have to stir the pasta every now and then as it cooks. Eminent American food science author Harold McGee writes in his book An Encyclopedia of Kitchen Science that you get the best results for spaghetti if you cook it in a frying pan, using cold water to start
